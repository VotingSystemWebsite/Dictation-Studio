How to Use Dictation Studio

  • Enter your text in the text area above
  • Select your preferred language and voice from the settings panel
  • Adjust speed, repeat settings, and pause duration to match your learning pace
  • Click "Start Dictation" or use the floating button to begin
  • The current word will be highlighted as it's spoken for visual tracking
  • Use pause/resume or stop buttons as needed during practice

Practical Use Cases

Language Learning: Practice pronunciation, improve listening comprehension, and build vocabulary in multiple languages.
Professional Development: Practice speeches, presentations, or technical terminology with controlled pacing and repetition.
Accessibility Tool: Assist users with reading difficulties or visual impairments by converting text to speech with visual tracking.
Memory Training: Use spaced repetition settings to improve memorization of texts, speeches, or study materials.
